Sweet Pepper Skillet

Decription:

I got this recipe from here.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Teaspoons of Olive Oil
  • 4 Thinly Sliced Peppers
  • 1 Medium Cut Onion
  • 2 Teaspoons of Minced Garlic
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of Pepper

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; saute peppers and onion until crisp-tender, 4-6 minutes. Add garlic, salt and pepper; cook and stir 1 minute.
