Vegan Pancakes

Decription:

I got this recipe from here. They do not have eggs in it so I approve. :)

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup of Flour
  • 1 Tablespoon of Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoons of Baking Powder
  • 1/8 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Cup of Soymilk
  • 2 Tablespoons of Vegetable Oil

Steps:

  1. Set a stove element with a pan to medium heat.
  2. Combine the 4 dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder -- two Tablespoons, not two Teaspoons as some have suggested, salt) in a bowl.
  3. Add the soy milk and vegetable oil to your mixture.
  4. Mix until smooth.
  5. Now the pan should be ready for your batter, so spoon one pancakes' worth of the mixture into the pan.
  6. Flip [carefully] when you see bubbles in the middle of the pancake, or if the edges are looking stiffened.
  7. Repeat until the batter is gone, and try not to eat them all while you're cooking them.
